## What Fintpe IS

A calm, organized view of your overall financial life.

### Core Features

1. **Net Worth Dashboard**
   - Add your accounts with current balances (not transactions)
   - Assets: bank balances, investments (MF, stocks, FD, PPF), property, gold, crypto
   - Liabilities: loans, credit cards, money owed
   - Update balances periodically (monthly/quarterly)
   - See net worth trend over time

2. **Document Vault**
   - Store/reference important documents: insurance policies, loan agreements, tax returns, property papers, wills
   - Tag, categorize, search
   - Know where everything is when you need it

3. **Reminders & Events**
   - Insurance renewal dates
   - Loan EMI schedules
   - Tax deadlines
   - FD/RD maturity dates
   - Premium due dates
   - Custom financial reminders

4. **Account Registry**
   - A simple list of all your accounts (banks, brokers, insurers)
   - Reference info: account numbers, nominees, contacts
   - Useful for you AND your family in emergencies

5. **Rate Charts & Calculators**
   - Quick reference for FD rates, PPF rates, etc.
   - Financial calculators (SIP, EMI, retirement, etc.)

## Design Principles

1. **Offline-first:** Works without internet after initial load
2. **No account required to explore:** Let users see value before signing up
3. **Minimal UI:** Big picture first, details on demand
4. **Periodic updates:** Designed for monthly check-ins, not daily use
5. **Mobile-friendly:** Most Indian users are mobile-first
