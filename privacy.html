Privacy Policy

Plain English. No legalese. Here's exactly how we handle your data.

Back to Home

TL;DR — The Short Version

Your financial data stays on your device. We don't see it, store it, or sell it. You're in complete control.

What Data Stays on Your Device

All of your sensitive financial information is stored locally on your device using browser localStorage. This includes:

  • Bank account balances and transactions
  • Investment holdings and performance
  • Budget and goal information
  • Expense categories and history
  • Net worth calculations
  • Any manually entered assets

This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to export it.

What We Don't Collect

We have no interest in your financial data. Here's what we will never collect:

  • Your transaction history
  • Your account balances
  • Your spending patterns
  • Your investment holdings
  • Your net worth
  • Your bank credentials
  • Your location data

What We May Collect (Optional)

If you choose to create an account for cloud sync, we collect minimal information:

  • Email address — only for account access and important updates
  • Encrypted backup data — if you opt into cloud backup

Optional Analytics

We may collect anonymized, aggregate usage data (like "feature X was used") to improve the product. This is opt-in and contains no personal or financial information.

No Third-Party Tracking

We don't use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services:

  • No Google Analytics
  • No Facebook Pixel
  • No advertising networks
  • No data brokers
  • No behavioral tracking

Your Data, Your Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • Export anytime: Download all your data as CSV or JSON
  • Delete anytime: Clear all local data with one click
  • No account required: Use the full app without signing up
  • Offline access: Works without internet connection

Security Measures

Even though we don't store your data, we take security seriously:

  • All connections use HTTPS encryption
  • No data is transmitted to external servers
  • Local storage uses browser security protections
  • Optional encrypted cloud backup (if enabled)

Cookies

We use minimal, essential cookies only:

  • Session management (if logged in)
  • Theme preference (dark/light mode)
  • No tracking cookies
  • No third-party cookies

Contact

Questions about privacy? We're happy to explain anything in more detail.

Email: privacy@fintpe.com

Our Promise

We built Fintpe because we were tired of finance apps that treat user data as a product. Your financial life is private. It should stay that way.
